Dear Sir/Madam

Season's Greetings from IIT-Jodhpur Campus Placement Team!!!

It gives me immense pleasure to invite you to participate in the Campus Placement Meet 2019-20 of the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur at our Academic Campus in Jodhpur. Our placement activities commences from July 2018 onwards.
The Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur, established in 2008 (spread across 852 acres of land),, has shown its glory and excellence by emerging as a leading Indian Institute, nurturing technical competence and a holistic development of its students. As on date, the Institute has 964 Students (641 B.Tech., 71 M.Tech., 97 M.Sc. and 155 Ph.D. students) along with 78 Faculty Members and 63 Staff Members. The Institute is committed to technology development and has established state–of–the–art laboratories for basic research. The degree programs are organized through 9 Departments.

The students in the graduating batch of IIT Jodhpur are keen to become a valuable part of your organization and would contribute to furthering your objectives. Our graduating batch strength including undergraduates and postgraduates will be 213 students (UG and PG) for the year 2019 -20. Highlights of the different courses are under mentioned:

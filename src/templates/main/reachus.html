  How to reach IIT Jodhpur?

  1. Where is IIT Jodhpur?
    2.

    IIT Jodhpur Campus is located at Karwar which will be 20 kms away from the center of the city of Jodhpur on an 852 acre land on National Highway 65 (NH65).

  2. How to reach IIT Jodhpur Campus?
    3.

    Jodhpur is connected well by road, rail and air to all major cities in the country, and by mega highways to major neighboring cities, e.g., Jaipur, Jaisalmer, Udaipur and Ahmedabad. 1. It is connected by rail to major cities in the country, like Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kota, Kanpur, Ahmedabad, Indore, Bhopal, Jabalpur, Guwahati, Nagpur, Lucknow and Jaipur; 1. There are daily flights from Delhi and Mumbai to Jodhpur. Air India and Jet Airways operate the daily flight to each of these cities; and 2. Jodhpur is connected by mega-highway to major neighboring cities, e.g., Jaipur, Jaisalmer, Udaipur and Ahmedabad. Deluxe and express buses ply to Jodhpur from Delhi, Jaipur, Ahmedabad and Baroda.

    1. From Airport to Permanent Campus:

      2. You can take a prepaid Auto/Taxi to the Institute, which is situated at NH65, Nagaur Road, Karwar-Village, Lavera Boari-Tehsil, Jodhpur 342037. You may ask the driver to take you to Nagaur Road. It is a about 26 km from Airport and will be 45 minute drive from airport to the Institute

    2. From Railway Station to Academic Campus

      3. At Jodhpur Junction railway station, exit from Platform No.1 side. You can take a prepaid Auto/Taxi to the Institute, which is situated at NH65, Nagaur Road, Karwar-Village, Lavera Boari-Tehsil, Jodhpur 342037. You may ask the driver to take you to Nagaur Road. It is a about 24 km from Airport and will be 30 minute drive from Railway Station to the Institute.

Season's Greetings from IIT-Jodhpur Campus Placement Team!!!

It gives me immense pleasure to invite you to participate in the Campus Placement Meet 2018-19 of the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur at our Academic Campus in Jodhpur. Our placement activities commences from July 2018 onwards. The pre-placement talks starts from August 2018, followed by campus recruitment from 1 st December 2018.
The Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur, established in 2008, has shown its glory and excellence by emerging as a leading Indian Institute, nurturing technical competence and a holistic development of its students. As on date, the Institute has 848 Students (555 B.Tech., 56 M.Tech., 71 M.Sc. and 166 Ph.D. students) along with 60 Faculty Members, 2 Visiting Faculty Members and 60 Staff Members. The Institute is committed to technology development, and has established state–of–the–art laboratories for basic research. We also have third year students who aspire to do internships at your prestigious firm.
